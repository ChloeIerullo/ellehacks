EATUP web and smart phone application:
We live in a society that while there are people who sleep hungry, restaurants throw their excess ingredients out every night! EATUP is a tool of communication between consumers and food donators that helps the environement by using all the natural resources that have been  used to make the food, so this way there is less food wasted, and also helps the health of those who that are going through hardship by providing them with all kinds of nutritions needed.

Prerequisites:
This webapp runs on any web browser
